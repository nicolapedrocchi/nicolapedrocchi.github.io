Titolo & Iterative Learning Procedure with Reinforcement for High-Accuracy Force Tracking in Robotized Tasks

Abstract & The paper focuses on industrial interaction robotics tasks, investigating a control approach involving multiple learning levels for training the manipulator to execute a repetitive (partially) changeable task, accurately controlling the interaction. The proposed approach consists of two main control levels based on compliance control: i) iterative friction learning compensation controller with reinforcement and ii) iterative force-tracking learning controller with reinforcement. The learning algorithms rely on iterative and reinforcement learning procedures to automatize the controllers’ parameter tuning. The proposed procedure has been applied to an automotive assembly task.

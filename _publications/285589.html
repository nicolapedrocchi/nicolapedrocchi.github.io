Titolo & Multi-robot spot-welding cells: An integrated approach to cell design and motion planning
Autori & Pellegrinelli, S.; Pedrocchi, N.; Molinari Tosatti, L.; Fischer, A.; Tolio, T.

Abstract & The necessity to manage several vehicle models on the same robotized assembly cell has made the cell design and the robot off-line motion planning two fundamental activities. Industrial practice and state-of-the-art methods focus on the technical issues of each activity. Still, no integrated approach has been proposed, resulting in a lack of optimality for the final cell configuration. The paper formalizes the whole process and proposes a heuristic multi-stage method for identifying the optimal combination of cell design choices and motion planning. The proposed architecture is depicted through a real case for welding applications.
